Site Name

The site name is Junior's Plaza. This name was selected as it reflects the focus on providing services and information relevant to our community. It embodies a sense of belonging and local commerce.

Optional domain availability: juniorplaza.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for community engagement by providing information about local events, business resources, and membership opportunities. It aims to foster connections among residents and businesses in the area.

Scenarios

  • What local events are happening this month?
  • How can I become a member of Junior's Plaza?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the site are:

Primary Color: Blue (#2980b9) - Used for headings.

Accent Color: Red (#e74c3c) - Used for buttons and highlights.

Secondary Color: Green (#2ecc71) - Used for body text.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure readability.
  • Akaya Kanadaka: Used for headings to add a unique flair.
